Koreanify Reference

Help:

Usage: Select writable text area (like the adress Bar), write your romanized text in said format, select the text by holding left button of mouse, press KEYWORD, Enjoy!

Keyword: CTRL + K

Romanized English Format: Syllables are separated by uppercase characters. Words are separated by standard whitespace.

Example:
jeoNeun MeoGeo => jeo neun | meo geo => 저는 머거
